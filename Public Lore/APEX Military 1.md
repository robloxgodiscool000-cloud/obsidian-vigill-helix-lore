# APEX Military

> [!summary] Overview
> **APEX Military** is the paramount armed division of StellarGen-APEX. Operating as a hybrid between a high-end defence contractor and a sovereign corporate army, they boast a technological supremacy that essentially outclasses the combined forces of the Rose Accord. They are the enforcers of StellarGen's will, deployed to protect corporate assets, secure the atmospheric scrubbers, and project overwhelming hard power across the globe.
## Organisational Structure
Similar to the rest of StellarGen’s subsidiaries, APEX Military operates under a rigid corporate-paramilitary hybrid structure designed to maximise global leverage, enforce market dominance, and guarantee humanity's security. Mirroring the hyper-efficient corporate giants of the pre-war era, the division treats tactical force as a quantifiable financial asset. It is led by a **Director-General of Global Security Operations**, a high-ranking executive who bridges the gap between the boardroom and the battlefield, answering directly to the APEX Board of Directors.
## Technological Supremacy 
APEX Military gear is instantly recognizable: sleek, minimalist, and terrifyingly efficient. Standing in stark contrast to the traditional armour and armaments of the armed forces of the Rose Accord, APEX utilises proprietary, high-end corporate technology.
Standard tactical gear is forged from lightweight, mist-resistant synthetic polymers, characterised by a sleek, black-chrome finish. Armour plating is modular, internally pressurised, and features active-camouflage dermal plating for urban environments.
Because their numbers are quite small, APEX heavily supplements its forces with automated, networked humanoid-drone swarms to hold outposts and patrol key areas.

## Operational Divisions
To efficiently police the safe zones and conquer the Wastelands, the military is divided into highly specialized branches:
* **[[Wall Infantry 1.md|Wall Infantry]]:** The largest branch, tasked with the gruelling, constant defence of the massive atmospheric scrubbers that protect the Remaining Nations that border the Red Wastelands. They repel feral mutant swarms and raider incursions daily.
* [[TRB)](Tactical Response Brigade (TRB|Tactical Response Brigade (TRB)]]%201.md): Elite, heavily armed strike teams deployed directly into the Red Wastelands. They act as the vanguard for NOVA research expeditions, securing high-value biological samples and terminating highly dangerous Mist anomalies.
