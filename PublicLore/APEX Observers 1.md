# APEX Observers

> [!summary] Overview
> **APEX Observers**, commonly referred to simply as **Observers**, are individuals who willingly sign a draconian, lifetime contract under [StellarGen-APEX](StellarGen-APEX%201.md) to be deployed to a NOVA Facility. Ostensibly presented as an employment opportunity offering safety, shelter, and steady meals, the contract carries a caveat: the corporation retains the absolute right to use them as expendable biological test subjects. 

## The Recruitment Pipeline
To ensure NOVA Facilities have a constant stream of fresh, new test subjects and prospective NOVA Employees, [StellarGen-APEX](StellarGen-APEX.md) acts as a filter and retriever. 

APEX recruiters frequently target the most vulnerable populations living within the [Remaining Nations](Remaining%20Nations.md). They prey on citizens crushed by [APEX Banking](StellarGen-APEX.md) debt, refugees seeking asylum from the fringes of [The Sanguine Wastelands](The%20Sanguine%20Wastelands.md), and dissidents given the choice between imprisonment by the [Corporate Security Department](Corporate%20Security%20Department.md) or a "corporate contribution contract." 

## The Promise vs. The Reality
The primary incentive for signing the Observer contract is survival. In a world where breathing clean air is a commodity, the promise of a permanent home inside a heavily fortified, climate-controlled corporate bunker is an intoxicating offer. 

However, upon arrival at a [StellarGen-NOVA](StellarGen-NOVA.md) site, the reality of their position is immediately enforced. Observers are stripped of their civilian rights and bodily autonomy. While some actually do end up being filtered into legitimate, low-level operational roles—such as janitorial staff for the [Technical & Maintenance Department](Technical%20&%20Maintenance%20Department.md) or basic lab assistants—they are ultimately classified as corporate property. 

## The Test Subjects
At any given moment, an Observer can be pulled from their daily duties and reassigned to the [Research & Medical Department](Research%20&%20Medical%20Department.md). Because NOVA operates completely outside of standard medical ethics, Observers are the primary human guinea pigs for the Facility's most dangerous projects. 

Their duties in this capacity include:
* Controlled exposure to [The Sanguine Mist](The%20Sanguine%20Mist.md) to test atmospheric scrubbers or new genetic immunities.
* Acting as live bait or behavioral baseline tests when interacting with captured [Mutants](Mutants.md).
* Subjecting their bodies to experimental cybernetics, volatile pharmaceuticals, and anomalous artifacts. 

If an Observer survives their tenure and proves exceptional aptitude, they *can* occasionally be promoted to full, protected NOVA Employees. Most, however, do not live long enough to see a promotion.
