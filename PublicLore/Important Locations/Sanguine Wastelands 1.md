# The Sanguine Wastelands

> [!summary] Overview
> **The Sanguine Wastelands** comprise the vast majority of the Earth's surface that has been entirely consumed by The Sanguine Mist. Held back by the bordering Walls, these regions are hyper-lethal, continually digested and biologically rewritten by the Mist. They serve as the undisputed domain of feral Mutants and AWOL Cells.

## Major Consumed Regions
While the Wastelands span the globe, cartographers have divided the collapsed territories into several distinct, highly specific hazard zones based on Mist density and geographical mutation.

### The Eurasian Red Steppe
Encompassing the former territories of **Russia, Eastern Europe, and Central Asia**, the Steppe is the largest unbroken stretch of Mist on the planet. The Mist here is incredibly dense, settling into the valleys and frozen tundras to create an ocean of red fog.

### The North American Deadlands
Spanning the **American Midwest, the Great Plains, and parts of rural Canada.** The plains are battered by colossal, continent-spanning Mist-storms. This chaotic, shifting environment is the primary hunting ground for AWOL Cells, who use the rolling storms to mask their massive crusades against corporate supply convoys travelling from the north down south.

### The Saharan Basin
Covering **Northern and Central Africa**, the intense heat and dry geography of the former desert have caused the Mist to calcify and crystallize. The region is a jagged, alien landscape of towering red bio-crystals and petrified dunes. It boasts the highest concentration of feral Mutants in the world, as the crystalline Mist rapidly accelerates extreme biological deviation. StellarGen-NOVA frequently launches high-casualty expeditions here to harvest genetic material.

### The Pacific Sanguine Veil
Encompassing the **Pacific Ocean, Oceania, and the entirety of the Australian Continent**, the Veil represents the Mist's interaction with open water. The Mist acts as a heavy, viscous blanket that hovers directly over the ocean surface, hyper-oxygenating the water and creating terrifying, leviathan-class marine mutants. Surface naval travel is entirely impossible, forcing the Remaining Nations and StellarGen to rely exclusively on flight to cross hemispheres.
