# Berlin

> [!summary] Overview
> Berlin is a sprawling, neon-futuristic metropolis located within Germany, a prominent member state of the Remaining Nations. As the historical birthplace of StellarGen, the city serves as the corporation's de facto global capital. Unlike much of the war-torn world, Berlin stands as a progressive, highly integrated sanctuary where humans and Mutants coexist under the direct protection and influence of StellarGen.

## The Neon Capital
Decades after the culmination of The Greatest War, Berlin has been entirely terraformed into a towering, vertical cyber-city. Sprawling blocks of hyper-dense skyscrapers, laced with cascading holographic advertisements and endless neon arrays, cut through the atmospheric haze. 

Because Germany belongs to the Rose Accord alliance, Berlin maintains political autonomy on paper. In practice, however, its infrastructure, economy, and security are deeply intertwined with StellarGen. Unlike hostile regimes that enforce immediate mutant purges, Berlin’s societal culture is deeply accepting. The local populace views StellarGen not as an occupying corporate force, but as the legitimate saviours of humanity and the architects of their prosperity.

## NOVA Site-01 "Headquarters"
At the heart of the city lies **NOVA Site-01**, colloquially known across the globe simply as "Headquarters." This architectural marvel is a massive, sprawling complex that anchors Berlin’s skyline. 

* Critically, Site-01 **does not conduct biological experimentation or Mist research**. Instead, it functions strictly as the central logistical, financial, and bureaucratic nervous system for both StellarGen-NOVA and StellarGen-APEX. 
* The facility serves as the primary global deployment hub for APEX Military. Furthermore, APEX’s numerous other corporate branches use Site-01 as their primary headquarters, managing everything from atmospheric wall manufacturing to good manufacturing.

## A Mutant Haven
Because StellarGen positions itself as the primary global protector of the mutated, its central hub is inherently a beacon for refugees. 
'Headquarters' employs thousands of stable mutants within its bureaucratic, security, and logistical sectors. This corporate integration has bled out into the city itself. Berlin boasts the highest demographic concentration of mutants of any metropolitan safe zone in the world. In the neon-lit streets, it is entirely commonplace to see mutated individuals working, socializing, and living alongside baseline humans, safe from the fires of the Red Wastelands or the purge squads of hostile nations.
