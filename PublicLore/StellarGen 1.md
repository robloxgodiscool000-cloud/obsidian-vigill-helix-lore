# StellarGen

> [!summary] Overview
> **StellarGen** is the undisputed corporate hegemony of the post-war era. While officially classified as a transnational megacorporation, it functions on a geopolitical scale as a technocratic global political player. Rather than pursuing malicious dominion, StellarGen operates under a clinical, absolute mandate: the preservation of human life and culture, the containment of environmental hazards that would threaten it, and the total eradication of the [Sanguine Mist](Sanguine%20Mist%201.md).

## Corporate Motives
StellarGen does not view itself as an evil empire, nor does it operate purely for corporate greed. Instead, its upper echelon views the corporation as the *sole competent steward of a dying planet*. Their ideology is built on absolute, unyielding utilitarianism. 
To StellarGen, traditional nation-states failed because they prioritized politics over survival during The Greatest War. Therefore, individual liberties, national borders, and ethical hesitation are seen as luxuries humanity can no longer afford. They believe that a heavily policed, highly structured corporate peace is the only alternative to absolute extinction. They protect and provide to Mutants and humans alike because *every single genome is an asset* to be cataloged, protected, and utilised in the war against the Mist.

## Historical Timeline

### 2028: The Foundation (Berlin)
Founded in Berlin, Germany, as a boutique biotech firm focusing on advanced genetic therapies, environmental cloning, and synthetic cellular structures. Its early years are defined by ground-breaking medical patents.

### 2040–2055: The Defence Contractor Shift
As global tensions escalate into the early phases of The Greatest War, StellarGen pivots heavily into military industrialism. They secure contracts with various superpowers to develop advanced bio-tech filters, localized atmospheric scrubbers, and gene-targeted medical trauma kits. 

### 2056: The Awakening of APEX and NOVA
Recognizing that the war is destabilizing global economies, the executive board restructures StellarGen into two distinct, specialized pillars: **APEX** (to generate immense capital and secure resources) and **NOVA** (to push the boundaries of pure, uninhibited biological science).

### 2060: The Sanguine Apocalypse & The Power Vacuum
The deployment of the Sanguine Mist triggers the collapse of traditional global governments. As nations fracture, StellarGen-APEX activates emergency contingency protocols. They rapidly construct the first generation of massive, walled refuges protected by their proprietary atmospheric scrubber technology. 

### 2061–2080: The Corporate Ascension
With the old world in ruins, billions of survivors trade their sovereignty to StellarGen in exchange for filtered air, clean water, and physical protection under APEX. StellarGen seamlessly transitions from a wartime contractor to a sovereign global entity, filling the power vacuum left by many fallen nations.

### 2085: The Current Era
StellarGen establishes hovering control over major metropolitan hubs like Berlin. They maintain a tense, cold-war relationship with the Rose Accord and actively hunt down cells of the AWOL Corps, all while funnelling trillions into the labs of StellarGen-NOVA to test the limits of known science.

## The Two Branches

### 1. [StellarGen-APEX](StellarGen-APEX.md)
APEX is the commercial, industrial, and military face of the corporation. It operates as a ruthless, highly efficient capitalistic machine designed to fund the rest of StellarGen's global infrastructure.
* APEX owns and operates the subsidiary companies that control the safe zones' energy grids, water filtration networks, heavy manufacturing, and atmospheric wall defence systems. 
* APEX Military, whilst acting as a major mercenary branch that is hired by various Remaining Nations to fight in petty disputes, also doubles as the ultimate iron fist of the Corporation. They respond to critical attacks and emergencies on NOVA Sites when the respective Security Department is overwhelmed.

### 2. [StellarGen-NOVA](StellarGen-NOVA.md)
NOVA is the primarily non-profit, purely scientific branch of the corporation. If APEX is the muscle and money, NOVA is the mind. Its structure is profoundly similar to a governmental agency. StellarGen-NOVA provides its Research personnel with the circumstances and powers to pursue their own research in order to benefit humanity within the corporate mandated limits.
* NOVA is tasked with the collection, containment, categorisation, and experimentation of all anomalies born from the Mist, including The Blood Eagle Strain and unique mutant biologies. 
* Operating out of Research Sites across the globe, NOVA is exempt from standard commercial pressures. It is all driven by a singular directive: find a permanent cure to rewrite the planet back to its pre-war state.
