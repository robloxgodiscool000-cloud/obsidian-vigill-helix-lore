# The Greatest War

> [!summary] Overview
> A devastating global conflict that irreparably altered the course of human history. The war culminated in the deployments of bioweapons which make up the [Sanguine Mist](Sanguine%20Mist%201.md), triggering a global apocalypse, the collapse of many nations, and the rise of [StellarGen](StellarGen%201.md) as a corporate hegemony.

## The Catalyst
Climate collapse and ecosystem failures triggered global shortages of freshwater, arable land, and rare-earth minerals required for advanced technology. Proxy conflicts escalated into direct warfare as major powers competed for resource corridors and strategic production zones.

## Escalation and Desperation
As the conflict dragged on, conventional warfare proved insufficient to break a grinding global stalemate. The traditional superpowers bled each other dry, draining their treasuries, infrastructure, and civilian populations. In a desperate bid to secure a definitive victory, international wartime treaties were abandoned, the United Nations was dissolved and, thus, opening the door for experimental, highly unstable weaponry. 

During this era, StellarGen originally founded in Berlin in 2028, became a premier military contractor and goods company, supplying advanced biotechnology through StellarGen-NOVA and supplying other solutions through StellarGen-APEX to desperate governments.

## The Sanguine Mist
The climax of The Greatest War was not a peace treaty, but an apocalypse. After months of constant deployments of various bioweapons, the self-replicating strains came to form a literal, spreading cloud of gas which began to spread across the globe.
* The weapon actively mutated the biospheres and the surviving human populace, creating the first generation of Mutants.
* Millions died in the initial strikes, and billions more perished as the Mist spread uncontrollably across the globe.

## The Aftermath
The deployment of the bioweapons completely shattered the infrastructure of the Old World. Global governments, already crippled by years of total war, collapsed under the catastrophic weight of the Mist. 

From the ashes of these fractured nations, [StellarGen](StellarGen%201.md) stepped into the power vacuum. By monopolising the only viable containment technology and offering refuge to the survivors, the corporation transitioned from a wartime contractor to an undisputed major player in the world. Today, the only factions like the Rose Accord (representing the Remaining Nations) and the [AWOL Corps](AWOL%20Corps.md) exist outside their influence.
