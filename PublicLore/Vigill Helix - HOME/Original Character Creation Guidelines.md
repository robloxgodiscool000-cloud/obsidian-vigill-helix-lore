An **Original Character (OC)** is a unique, custom persona created by a player to participate in the ongoing story of Vigill Helix. 
To maintain the integrity, consistency, and realism of the setting, there are a few regulations you must follow before interacting with the lore in-character.

### Starting Roles

- Depending on how you joined the community, your OC will begin as either an **APEX Observer** or a standard **Employee**. StellarGen strictly employs individuals based on their suitability and competence to carry out corporate duties.
### Origins & Species

1. Your OC must originate from Vigill Helix's Earth. Characters from other planets, alternate dimensions, or different realities are strictly prohibited.

2. Your OC must fall into one of the following categories:
    
    - **Humans:** Standard, baseline humanity.
        
    - **Mutants:** Humans altered by exposure to the Sanguine Mist, or those who have inherited genetic mutations from their parents.
        
    - **Robotics:** Synthetic entities, including sentient or non-sentient machines and AI.
        

### **Factions & Behaviour**

- Your OC may not be an active operative, operator, or sleeper agent for the AWOL Corps. You may, however, play as a sympathiser to an AWOL Corps Cell, however, **you will be subjected to _In Character_ regulations.**

- Your OC must demonstrate a realistic value for their own life to begin with.

- Your OC cannot be designed as an active, unprovoked, or immediate threat to other characters.
