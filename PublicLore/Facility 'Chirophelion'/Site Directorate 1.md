# The Directorate

> [!summary] Overview
> **The Directorate** sits at the absolute apex of the Facility’s hierarchy. Tasked with the ultimate goal of ensuring site integrity, operational efficiency, and absolute loyalty to StellarGen, this executive body dictates every major decision within the walls. Uniquely, the command structure is split evenly between a Director and a Co-Director.

## The Command Structure
Operating as a unified dual-mandate, the Director and Co-Director oversee the entire Facility. They hold the highest security clearances on-site, acting as the sole liaisons between the Facility's localised departments. To maintain their iron grip on the Facility's operations, two highly specialised subsidiaries report directly to their office, bypassing the Board of Directors.

## The Administration Office (AO)
The Administration Office is the bureaucratic and logistical engine of the Facility. While the Director and Co-Director make the grand strategic decisions, the AO executes them. 
* They manage the budgets distributed by APEX Banking, rationing power, funding, and supplies to the Research & Medical Department (R&MD) and the Technical & Maintenance Department (T&MD).
*  The AO handles the onboarding, assignments, and payroll of all Observers and employees stationed at the Facility, ensuring optimal worker efficiency at all times.

## Corporate Intelligence Services (CIS)
If the Enforcer Platoon of the Corporate Security Department acts as the Facility's police, the CIS acts as the Directorate's personal secret police. 
* CIS operators are deeply embedded within the Facility's own staff. They monitor communications, flag insubordination, and actively hunt for AWOL Corps sympathizers.
* They gather classified intelligence on external threats, interrogate high-value captives, and provide the Directorate with the data necessary to maintain absolute control over the site.
