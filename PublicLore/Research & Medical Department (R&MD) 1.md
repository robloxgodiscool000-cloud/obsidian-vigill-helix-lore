# Research & Medical Department (R&MD)

> [!summary] Overview
> The **Research & Medical Department (R&MD)** is the intellectual and biological lifeblood of Facility Chirophelion. Acting as the local operational crux for StellarGen-NOVA, the department is bifurcated into two distinct but highly cooperative branches. Their combined mandate is to maintain the health of all corporate assets on-site while relentlessly pushing the boundaries of human scientific achievement.

## The Medical Branch
The Medical Branch operates with a singular, pragmatic goal: providing comprehensive medical, psychological, and surgical care to all personnel within the Facility's walls. 
* The level of care is strictly universal across the Facility. All Observers and employees receive utilitarian trauma care and triage.
* Medical personnel are heavily trained in treating specialized injuries unique to the Facility, ranging from cybernetic rejection and industrial accidents to acute Sanguine Mist exposure and psychological trauma.

## The Research Branch
The Research Branch is the theoretical and experimental core of the Facility. In absolute alignment with StellarGen-NOVA’s overarching planetary directive, their primary focus is the study, containment, and ultimate eradication of The Sanguine Mist.

* The bulk of the branch’s funding goes toward analysing Mist strains, and developing new atmospheric countermeasures or biological immunities.
* While the Mist is the priority, the Research Branch is not limited to it. Depending on strict approval from internal R&MD higher-ups, scientists are permitted to pioneer studies in various other fields.
