# The Castellans

> [!summary] Overview
> The **Castellans**, officially designated as the APEX Wall Infantry, are the most numerous and heavily deployed branch of the [[APEX Military]]. Garrisoned atop the colossal atmospheric walls that encircle the safe regions, they act as the grim, unyielding shield between humanity and The Sanguine Mist. They endure a fatalistic, grueling existence, staring daily into an apocalyptic abyss to ensure the cities behind them survive.

## The Long Watch
To be a Castellan is to accept a life of bleak, endless vigilance. Much like the doomed sentinels of ancient myth standing before the gates of hell, these soldiers spend their deployments perched on towering barricades, peering into the shifting, red oceans of the The Sanguine Wastelands. 

Unlike the pristine, polished security forces of the Remaining Nations who police the regions at the very feet of the walls, Castellans are weathered, hardened, and deeply insular. They share a fierce, unspoken brotherhood forged in misery. They know that the oblivious citizens living their neon-lit, cyberpunk lives below only breathe because the Castellans hold the line.
## Gear and Arsenal
Because they operate on the absolute fringe of the safe zones, Castellans are subjected to localised Mist-blowback and extreme weather conditions. Their technology reflects this harsh reality.
* Their armour is bulky, heavily reinforced against biological corrosion, and constantly battered. Every suit is equipped with military-grade rebreathers and localised micro-scrubbers.
* The Mist actively defies standard surveillance, so Castellans utilise specialized thermal and sonar-imaging visors designed to pierce the dense red fog and spot thermal blooms moving in the wastes. 
* They do not engage in precision urban combat. Their doctrine is overwhelming, sustained firepower. They rely on heavy artillery emplacements, and rail-snipers for picking off threats.

## The Endless Siege
A Castellan's watch is never truly quiet. The walls are under constant, attritional siege from the horrors of the Wastelands.
* The most common threat is the relentless assault by Mutants. Driven mad by the Mist, massive bio-horrors frequently attempt to scale the walls or smash the automated intake vents of the atmospheric scrubbers.
* Above all, their greatest enemy is the environment itself. The Castellans must constantly monitor the wall's structural integrity, as the Sanguine Mist actively tries to digest the concrete and steel that holds it back.
As a result of this, the Castellans routinely visit nearby NOVA Sites to ensure security.
