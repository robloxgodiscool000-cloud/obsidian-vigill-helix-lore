# The Sanguine Mist

> [!summary] Definition
> The **Sanguine Mist** is a highly unstable, self-replicating collection of bioweapons deployed at the climax of The Greatest War. It largely manifests as a dense, crimson fog that aggressively rewrites the DNA of any biological matter it touches, resulting in mass casualties, the creation of Mutants, and the complete destabilisation of the ecosystems it occupies. There are a multitude of strains, each exhibiting unique traits. It is central to the research conducted by StellarGen-NOVA.

## Physical Properties and Behaviour
The Sanguine Mist acts as a hyper-dense, heavy particulate aerosol. It exhibits a deep crimson coloration and behaves with pseudo-sentient fluidity, constantly shifting and expanding to fill available space. High concentrations are easily identified by a distinct, heavy olfactory profile resembling oxidized copper.
Because the Mist is a cocktail of highly variable, self-replicating bioweapons, expansion rates differ wildly between samples. Crucially, the constituent micro-agents are omni-phagic; they aggressively break down and consume all surrounding matter, organic and inorganic, using the raw material to continuously synthesize new Mist.

## Ecological Impact
Prolonged exposure to the Sanguine Mist has actively terraformed vast swaths of the globe into hyper-lethal zones. Atmospheric saturation permanently occludes natural sunlight, casting a perpetual, hazy crimson pall over these regions.
Indigenous flora and fauna suffered a near-total extinction event. The majority of organic mass was broken down to synthesize further bioweapons, while surviving organisms underwent radical, grotesque mutations into unrecognisable anomalies. Furthermore, the Mist's omni-phagic properties have severely eroded the regions' physical topography. Decades of exposure have measurably reduced the altitude of natural mountain ranges, and sprawling pre-war metropolitan centres have been digested into structurally unstable, decaying ruins.

## StellarGen
Operating under the self-appointed mandate of global salvation, StellarGen allocates unprecedented resources to combating the Sanguine Mist. Their operational doctrine is bifurcated into two primary divisions: Containment and Research.

### StellarGen-APEX
APEX serves as the infrastructural and militant vanguard against the Mist. Its subsidiary companies maintain the colossal walls of atmospheric scrubbers that secure the perimeter of every refuge. Concurrently, APEX Military is deployed to repel hostile incursions along these critical borders. APEX also commands heavily armed expeditions into the Red Wastelands, co-ordinating with NOVA to extract biological samples and neutralise hyper-lethal mutated fauna.

### StellarGen-NOVA (Research & Eradication)
NOVA directs the corporation's scientific initiatives. Operating out of a vast network of heavily fortified Research Sites, NOVA's primary objective is the complete biological eradication of the Mist. This goal is pursued through relentless scientific experimentation.

## Symptoms of Lethal Exposure
Direct contact with most strains within the Sanguine Mist is lethal almost immediately. **Symptoms include:**
* **Acute Pulmonary Liquefaction:** Inhalation causes the micro-agents to rapidly dissolve lung tissue in order to synthesize new Mist, resulting in immediate asphyxiation.
    
- **Spontaneous Cellular Necrosis:** Dermal contact triggers aggressive degradation; the Mist strips organic matter at a cellular level, causing severe, burn-like tissue loss.
    
- **Total Biomass Conversion:** Post-mortem, the subject's remaining organic mass is violently broken down and expelled as a fresh cloud of the Sanguine Mist.
