# Corporate Security Department

> [!summary] Overview
> The **Corporate Security Department (CSD)** is the armed defensive spine of the Facility. Operating under a mandate of zero-tolerance, their ultimate goal is the absolute protection of the Facility from all external breaches and internal hazards, as well as safeguarding the personnel within its walls. The department’s roster ranges from menial checkpoint sentries to highly trained tactical Operators.

## Department Structure
To maintain absolute control over the Facility's vast operations, the CSD is strictly divided into three specialised branches:

### 1. The Guardsmen Platoon
The Guardsmen act as the first line of defense and the most visible security presence in the Facility. Comprised of standard corporate security personnel, their duties are absolutely essential. They man the metaphorical walls, conduct patrols, monitor surveillance, and enforce basic access-level clearances. They are the shield that keeps daily operations running smoothly.

### 2. The Enforcer Platoon
Serving as the Facility's military police, the Enforcers are responsible for internal discipline and corporate law. They do not watch the perimeter exclusively; they also watch the personnel. Enforcers investigate corporate espionage (such as Sub-Rosa infiltrations), handle staff insubordination, and neutralise overt leakage of classified information.

### 3. Riot Control Unit (RCU)
The RCU is the elite tactical spearhead of the CSD. Heavily armoured and armed with high-grade [[StellarGen-APEX]] weaponry, these Operators are deployed commonly when standard security fails. Their primary function is lethal pacification and heavy containment. Whether quelling a massive uprising, neutralising a terrifying specimen breach in a Research wing, or simply escorting a High Valued Target, they are a force to be reckoned with.
