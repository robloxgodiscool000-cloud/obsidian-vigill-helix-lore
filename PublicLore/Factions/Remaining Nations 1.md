# The Remaining Nations

> [!summary] Overview
> **The Remaining Nations** are the surviving sovereign political entities of the old world. While they still maintain their borders and local governments within the safe zones, their actual autonomy is completely dictated by their relationship with StellarGen and **The Rose Accord.**
---

## StellarGen Allies 
These nations have entirely surrendered their political sovereignty to StellarGen. Their local governments are largely figureheads, and their economies are completely integrated into the APEX Banking credit standard.

* **Germany:** The absolute crown jewel of corporate territory. It hosts NOVA Site-01 (Berlin) and has completely phased out its independent military in favour of automated APEX Military protection. 
* **Japan:** A hyper-advanced nation that willingly dissolved its old government. It operates as a direct R&D extension for StellarGen-NOVA, specialising in high-end cybernetic consumer goods and corporate surveillance infrastructure.

## Rose Accord Allies/Members
These notable countries fiercely resist corporate encroachment, and maintain varying degrees of a relationship with the Rose Accord. Despite this, they collectively fund the Rose Accord's military assets and work tirelessly to find a way to survive without completely relying on StellarGen's technology.

## Dual-Aligned Territories 
These nations are caught in a diplomatic dead-zone. Politically and culturally, they align with the Rose Accord's message of human freedom, but practically, they are utterly dependent on StellarGen to keep their international prestige.

* **The United States of America:** Despite remaining a top player in the Rose Accord and housing Detroit within its borders, the USA is culturally torn. While its politicians vote in lockstep with the Rose Accord at global summits, its massive manufacturing lines are entirely contracted to APEX. They cannot leave the corporate network without triggering an immediate economic collapse.
