# The Rose Accord

> [!summary] Overview
> **The Rose Accord,** or the **World Federation for Global Democracy** is the fragile, governing political alliance that unites the [Remaining Nations](Remaining%20Nations.md). Formed in the catastrophic aftermath of The Greatest War, the Accord was established to preserve the last vestiges of human sovereignty, democracy, and national identity. Today, it acts as the only unified political bloc attempting to resist the actions of rebel cells, the Sanguine Mist and the StellarGen hegemony.

## Formation and Purpose
When the Sanguine Mist blanketed the globe and traditional superpowers collapsed, the surviving governments realized that fighting each other was no longer viable. The leaders of the surviving territories signed the Rose Accord in *2061*, pledging mutual defence, resource sharing, and open borders to ensure human survival. 

The Accord operates out of a centralised parliament based in [Detroit](Detroit.md). They view themselves as the legitimate successors to pre-war humanity, desperately trying to maintain laws, culture, and human rights in a world that has largely forgotten or forsaken them.

## The Dynamic
The defining characteristic of the Rose Accord is the Remaining Nations crippling dependancy on StellarGen. Because the Accord lacks the advanced technological advancements of StellarGen-NOVA or the manufacturing monopoly of StellarGen-APEX, the member Nations rely entirely on APEX-built atmospheric scrubbers to survive the Mist.

The Accord constantly attempts to pass international laws to sanction StellarGen, limit APEX Military deployments in sovereign borders, and demand open-source access to the scrubber technology. In response, StellarGen routinely ignores these laws. When the Accord pushes too hard, APEX Banking simply raises the interest rates on the maintenance of the atmospheric scrubbers, threatening to let entire cities suffocate if they do not comply.

Due to the sporadic nature of the diplomatic status between the two global giants, the Rose Accord may sometimes find themselves in proxy wars against StellarGen or may find themselves working together to face a far greater threat.

## WFGD Armed Forces/Rosen Soldiers
Unlike the hyper-elite, mostly cybernetically augmented troops of the APEX Military, the military wing of the Rose Accord is built on grit and the numbers of Remaining Nations who donate troops to the cause.

The WFGD Armed Forces regularly deploy in various conflicts throughout the world, either disconnected from StellarGen entirely such as due to some Remaining Nations' petty wars or direct attacks on NOVA Sites and APEX mining sites, military compounds and more.
Due to the nature of the Rosen Soldiers, in that they range from the globe's various militaries, countless battalions scale differently in terms of technology and weaponry. Largely instead, they rely on entrenched fortifications, heavy artillery, and sheer manpower to fulfil their orders.

Whilst the WFGD Armed Forces outnumber APEX Military to an outrageous degree, because the Accord Military is chronically outgunned, they occasionally employ AWOL Corps cells as deniable elite mercenaries to strike APEX Expedition convoys and NOVA technology.
