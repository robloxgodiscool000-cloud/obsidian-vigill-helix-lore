# The Red Butchers

> [!summary] Overview
> **The Red Butchers** are a massive, fanatical cult that worships The Sanguine Mist as a divine unifier and biological remaker. Operating technically as the largest and most radicalised cell within the AWOL Corps network, they are a terrifying hybrid of armed pragmatic insurgents, religious zealots and peaceful worshippers. Their ranks are uniquely integrated, consisting of both humans and mutants alike. 

## Ideology and Induction
While other factions fight to survive the Mist, the Butchers embrace it. They view the genetic rewriting caused by the Mist as the next necessary step in human evolution, a great equaliser that strips away class, borders, and corporate greed. Their primary primary activity is proselytisation. Cult infiltrators preach to the destitute, starving, and hopeless populations within the lower classes of the Remaining Nations. They act as dark shepherds, smuggling willing converts outside the walls and into the Red Wastelands to be "inducted."
## Arsenal and Tactics
The Red Butchers completely lack high-tech weaponry. They view cybernetics and bio-filtration as blasphemy against the Mist.

Their most terrifying tactical advantage is their integration with the Wasteland's horrors. Fully mutated cultists fight seamlessly alongside human initiates, using their bio-crystalline armor and Mist-adapted bodies as living shields against corporate gunfire.

## The Crusades
For the most part, the Butchers operate in the shadows, quietly siphoning citizens into the Mist or launching targeted raids on isolated StellarGen-NOVA research sites to "liberate" captured mutants. 

However, on rare occasions, their leaders will declare a formal **"Crusade."** During a Crusade, thousands of Butchers and enraged mutants form a massive, unstructured horde, throwing themselves with suicidal devotion against StellarGen walls and APEX defense lines in a bloody, apocalyptic attempt to tear down the atmospheric scrubbers and let the Mist inherit the earth.
