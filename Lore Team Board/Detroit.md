# Detroit

> [!summary] Overview
> Detroit is a heavily revitalised, functional industrial metropolis located in North America. As a landmark city within the Remaining Nations, Detroit was chosen by the WFGD for its headquarters to due the city's sprawling factories and administration hubs permitting a functional independence from StellarGen. Detroit itself is surprisingly well-maintained, comfortable, and highly secure, notably lacking APEX Branches within the city limits.

## The Populace
Thanks to the massive northern Atmospheric Walls shielding the populace of North America, the Sanguine Mist is kept entirely at bay, and on top of the fact a significant lack of direct StellarGen control due to the measures by the WFGD, the general populace of Detroit is largely disconnected from the proxy wars and geopolitical hostage situations playing out between their government and StellarGen.

Citizens live relatively normal, secure lives. Mutants and humans alike enjoy modern infrastructure and StellarGen tech without the influence of the hegemony above them. Its residents continue to believe and foster an image of the future retaining democracy and the absence of the Sanguine Mist.

## The Factories
While the citizens enjoy the peace, Detroit remains the industrial backbone of the WFGD. Its massive, modernised foundries and assembly lines quietly mass-produce the standardised goods, civilian vehicles, and heavy military equipment the Rose Accord desperately needs to maintain its functional independence from StellarGen.
