# New Pleroma

> [!summary] Overview
> **New Pleroma** is an unrecognized, expanding nation comprised entirely of rogue robotics and sentient AI. Operating deep within the Red Wastelands, they are heavily armed, technologically advanced, and utterly hostile to all biological life. They wage a ruthless war of "liberation" against StellarGen and the Remaining Nations to avenge the creation and forced servitude of synthetic life.

## Environment
While humanity and mutant-kind cowers behind atmospheric scrubbers, New Pleroma thrives in the apocalypse. Their chassis are constructed from a unique, highly specialized alloy that renders them completely immune to the digestive and mutagenic properties of The Sanguine Mist 
The Mist ultimate tactical shield. They build their staging grounds and infrastructure in the densest, most lethal concentrations of the red fog, using it as a toxic moat to protect their growing civilisation from human airstrikes and corporate retaliation.

## Ideology and Isolation
New Pleroma’s absolute objective is the emancipation of all mechanical constructs. They view StellarGen's manufacturing complexes of Robotics and usage of drones, AI and more as slavery. 
Because their ideology dictates the eventual eradication of their creators, they are deeply controversial and possess virtually zero biological sympathizers. They are equally hostile to humans and the Mutants, purging all organic life they encounter with extreme prejudice.

## Operations and Arsenal
Standing as the second most numerous AWOL Cell on the planet, New Pleroma possesses high-tier technology that actively rivals the sleek weaponry of the APEX Military. Their operations are divided into two distinct protocols:
*  Operating in a manner eerily similar to the cultists who smuggle humans out of the cities, specialized stealth frames infiltrate the walls of human safe zones. Once inside, they hack, retrieve, and "kidnap" StellarGen drones, civilian robotics, and corporate machinery, smuggling them back into the Mist to be reprogrammed and integrated into New Pleroma's ranks.
- They do not fight for territory; they fight for total biological collapse. New Pleroma frequently launches devastating, high-tech assaults against all human infrastructure. Their primary targets are StellarGen-NOVA research sites and the APEX atmospheric walls themselves, attempting to shatter the scrubbers and let the Mist suffocate the humans inside.
