# Civilian Life in the Safe Zones

> [!summary] Overview
> Contrary to the apocalyptic wasteland beyond the walls, **the average civilian life** is remarkably comfortable and highly advanced. Shielded from The Sanguine Mist by massive atmospheric scrubbers, the average citizen enjoys a vibrant, high-tech existence. The true existential stress of the modern era is not survival, but navigating the omnipresent political cold war between StellarGen and the The WFGD.

## A "Bright" Existence
Whilst the Remaining Nations differ sporadically in quality of life and economic success, life usually within them resembles a highly functional, sanitised cyber-metropolis.
* Cybernetic enhancements are commonplace and accessible to the middle class, courtesy of StellarGen technology.
* For the majority of the population, the stemmed tide of the Sanguine Mist is something they only see on the evening news.

## The Great Political Divide
Because a large portion of the populace is insulated from the horrors of the Wastelands and the physical violence of factions like the AWOL Corps or New Pleroma, civilian culture is entirely consumed by politics. Society is deeply, aggressively polarised by the rivalry between the two reigning superpowers.

* A massive portion of the populace supports StellarGen. They view the corporation's StellarGen-APEX division as the ultimate saviour of humanity, arguing that without corporate technology and the APEX Banking standard, the walls would have fallen decades ago. They embrace corporate branding, rely heavily on APEX technology, and view the Rose Accord as an outdated, ungrateful relic.
* Conversely, a fiercely vocal demographic aligns with the Rose Accord. They view StellarGen as a tyrannical monopoly holding humanity hostage through unpayable debt and synthetic dependence. They advocate for human sovereignty, governmental oversight, and the reclamation of the world from corporate hands.
