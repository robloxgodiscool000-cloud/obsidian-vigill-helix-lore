# APEX Banking

> [!summary] Overview
> **APEX Banking** is the disputed financial spine of the modern era. Following the collapse of many traditional fiat currencies during The Greatest War, this division established sizeable control over the global economy. It functions as a weaponised financial institution, utilising debt, currency monopoly, and capital leverage to underwrite StellarGen's global operations. The only entities that maintain closed, separate economies are select Remaining Nations, the Rose Accord and the AWOL Corps.

## Key Operations

* **APEX Banking** issues and regulates the primary digital currency used across the web and many Remaining Nations. It is the primary currency in all NOVA Sites. By backing this currency entirely with StellarGen-APEX assets and StellarGen-NOVA produced technology, StellarGen ensures that APEX Finance remains the top financial institution in the world.
* The division ruthlessly exploits the financial vulnerabilities of the Remaining Nations to ensure StellarGen's ultimate goal is left uninhibited. When members of the WFGD fall behind on loans and fails to uphold grant terms, StellarGen moves in to reassert control.
- APEX Banking acts as a primary pipeline for the corporation's capital. It strips profits from internal commercial markets and channels trillions of credits directly into the research projects of StellarGen-NOVA.
