# The Crazies

> [!summary] Overview
> The **Tactical Response Brigade (TRB)**, universally referred to as **"The Crazies,"** are among the apex predators of the APEX Military. Deployed only when catastrophic Wall breaches occur or high-tier corporate assets are under threat, they are heavily augmented, heavily armoured transhumans. Stripped of their humanity and operating with terrifying, synchronised brutality, they are the shortsword of APEX Military when the spear fails.

## The Transhuman Procedures
To join the TRB is to forfeit humanity. Recruits, often the most lethal and psychologically detached operators in the corporate military, undergo a radical, invasive cybernetic conversion that borders on sanctioned psychosis. 

Their nervous systems are interwoven with APEX combat subroutines, suppressing fear, pain, and hesitation. Most terrifyingly, their organic vocal cords are surgically removed and replaced with broadband tactical modulators. To baseline humans, their communication sounds like bursts of distorted radio static.

## Arsenal and Appearance
The Crazies are physically imposing, their bodies expanded by synthetic musculature and encased in seamless, faceless heavy armour. 
* They lack traditional visors. Instead, their helmets are blank, armoured domes packed with LIDAR, Mist-penetrating optics, and a constant feed of tactical telemetry. 
* Because their cybernetics absorb recoil that would shatter a human arm, they wield heavy, rapid-fire boltguns, hyper-vibrating blades, and concussive explosives as standard infantry arms. 
## The Apex Predators
The TRB is not a defensive force; they are an absolute obliteration protocol. They are typically deployed via heavily armoured hover-gunships directly into the most hopeless combat zones.

When a colossal mutant tears down a section of the Wall, or a Red Butchers Crusade overwhelms the Castellans, the TRB is sent in. Their combat doctrine is inhumanly fast, hyper-aggressive, and entirely devoid of self-preservation. They move in perfect, silent coordination, guided by their localised digital hive-mind.
