# Sub-Rosa

> [!summary] Overview
> **Sub-Rosa** is a highly clandestine, deeply entrenched terrorist cell operating under the broader umbrella of the AWOL Corps in StellarGen's log. Sub-Rosa is driven entirely by greed. Their ultimate goal is to dismantle the [[StellarGen]] monopoly piece by piece, filling their own pockets in the ensuing chaos. Their name is not their official name, but rather a title affixed by StellarGen.

## Operations
Sub-Rosa is by far the smallest faction within the AWOL network, but their lack of numbers is their greatest asset. They are practically unknown to the general public, and even high-ranking NOVA Site directors consider them little more than a ghost story. 
They do not hold territory, and they do not fight in the trenches. Instead, Sub-Rosa relies entirely on asymmetrical cyber-warfare, deep-cover infiltration, and a vast network of sleeper agents embedded directly within StellarGen’s infrastructure and the cities of the Remaining Nations.

## Benefactors
While StellarGen officially classifies them as apex-level terrorists, Sub-Rosa's most frequent employer is actually the The Rose Accord. Because the Accord cannot legally strike StellarGen without risking global suffocation, they hire Sub-Rosa heavily off-the-books. Sub-Rosa acts as the Accord's darkest scalpel, performing highly illegal corporate sabotage in exchange for exorbitant, untraceable sums of capital.
