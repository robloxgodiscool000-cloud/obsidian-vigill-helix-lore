# AWOL Corps

> [!summary] Overview
> The **AWOL Corps** is an highly decentralised syndicate of rogue mercenaries, military defectors, black-market operators and radical terrorists. Despite the name, the AWOL Corps are not a unified group. 'AWOL Corps' is a broad, catch-all term to describe all cells that oppose StellarGen, though their respective reasons often differ sporadically.

## Origins and Identity
The Corps is primarily comprised of soldiers, workers, and drifters who went "Absent Without Official Leave" from the corporate structure of the world. Due to the nature of AWOL, practically all cells differ.
