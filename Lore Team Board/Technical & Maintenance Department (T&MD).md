# Technical & Maintenance Department (T&MD)

> [!summary] Overview
> The **Technical & Maintenance Department (T&MD)** is the mechanical and digital backbone of the Facility. Operating in conjunction with sterile environments and the depths of claustrophobic maintenance tunnels, their ultimate goal is to ensure that the site’s vast array of technology, heavy machinery, and Artificial Intelligence networks function flawlessly. They are the blue-collar workmen and women who keep the corporate fortress breathing.

## Heavy Infrastructure & Machinery
The physical survival of the Facility relies entirely on the T&MD. These engineers and technicians are responsible for the constant upkeep of the site's most critical hardware. 
## The Backbone
Unlike the elite scientists of the R&MD or the armed Operators of the CSD, T&MD personnel are the grease-stained, overworked laborers of the Facility. They spend their shifts in the claustrophobic maintenance shafts, conducting routine inspections, and more. While they rarely receive the glory or the high-tier privileges of the other departments, any total failure of the T&MD would result in the immediate collapse of the Facility.
