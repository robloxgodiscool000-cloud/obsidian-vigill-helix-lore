# The Blood Eagle Strain

> [!summary] Overview
> The **Blood Eagle** is a localised, hyper-aggressive variant of the Sanguine Mist. Unlike the dominant strain which instantly dissolves baseline organic matter, the Blood Eagle acts as a rapid parasitic mutagen. It hijacks the host's nervous system and violently reshapes their biomass within hours, turning fauna into feral, zombie-like anomalies characterised by gruesome, wing-like biological eruptions.

## The Infection Process
When local fauna, humans and mutants are exposed to a pocket of the Blood Eagle strain, the bioweapon does not immediately kill them. Instead, the micro-agents flood the bloodstream and target the brain and skeletal structure. 
* **Incubation:** Within 1 to 3 hours, the host experiences acute mania, skyrocketing body temperature, and a complete loss of higher brain function. 
* **Eruption:** As the virus rapidly rewrites the host's DNA to weaponise the body, the host's ribcage and thoracic spine are violently forced outward, tearing through the skin of the back. 

## Physical Morphology
The signature characteristic of the Blood Eagle is this grotesque, winged silhouette. The bioweapon aggressively generates thick, fleshy growths and pulsating tumorous sacks along the ruptured back and shoulders. Within minutes of erupting, parts of this fleshy mass calcify into jagged, crimson **bio-crystalline structures**. 
These crystals jut out through the skin like ragged, bloody wings, acting as crude biological armor or deadly natural claws, locking the host's body into a permanent, agonising state of mutation.

## Observed Threat Variants
Because the Blood Eagle weaponises whatever biomass is available, the resulting abominations vary wildly based on the host's original size. StellarGen-APEX has categorised the most common variants for field operatives:

* **The Swarmers:** The most common result of infection. These are standard fauna whose metabolisms have been supercharged. They are incredibly fast, hyper-aggressive, and hunt in packs, using their jagged crystalline teeth to tear prey apart. Their "wings" are usually small, emerging crystal shards.
* **The Talons:** Subjects whose mutations focused heavily on skeletal restructuring. The bones in their forelimbs lengthen and erupt through the skin, coated in hardened bio-crystal to form massive, scythe-like claws capable of shredding tactical armor. 
* **The Goliaths:** Subjects that underwent catastrophic muscular hypertrophy. The strain forces their muscle mass to expand so violently that it tears their skin apart, creating towering "muscle monsters." Their immense bulk is heavily plated with thick crystalline armor, making them incredibly resistant to small-arms fire.
